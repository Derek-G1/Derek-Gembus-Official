## 1) Services
Provider will perform services described in a Statement of Work (SOW). If a term conflicts, the SOW controls for that project.

## 2) Fees & Payment
Fees are defined per SOW. Client is responsible for third‑party costs unless explicitly included.

## 3) Change Requests
Work outside the agreed scope requires written approval (change order) and may affect timeline and cost.

## 4) Confidentiality
Both parties agree to keep non-public business information confidential.

## 5) Intellectual Property
Unless otherwise stated in the SOW:
- Client owns final deliverables upon full payment.
- Provider retains rights to pre-existing tools, libraries, and generic code.

## 6) Warranties & Limitations
Provider will perform services in a professional manner. Except as required by law, services are provided “as is” and liability is limited to fees paid under the relevant SOW.

## 7) Termination
Either party may terminate with written notice. Client pays for work completed up to termination and any non-refundable deposits as defined in the SOW.

## 8) Disputes
Good-faith negotiation first; then mediation/arbitration or small claims depending on project size.
